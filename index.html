Connecting directly

To make changes to the DronePi autonomy software, you can connect via SSH or VNC by clicking on the links below.

This assumes you have a compatible SSH/VNC client on this device

  • SSH:es410@dronepi.local
  • VNC (native):dronepi.local; Username:es410
  • RealVNC (v6.22.826+):dronepi.local; Username:es410
